# Clara Mendes Product Sourcing Shortlist

Clara Mendes is positioned as a curated home goods store: quiet objects,
natural texture, soft lighting, ceramics, storage, textiles, and small accents.
The storefront should not surface formalwear, health supplements, skin care,
phone accessories, or generic personal-care products.

## Current Catalog Decision

Keep or prioritize:

- Drawer Reset Bundle: fits storage, bathroom reset, and home rituals.
- Soft Reset Candle: fits scent, atmosphere, and gifting.
- The Home Ritual Warmer: fits lighting, home ritual, and small-appliance decor.

Hide from the Clara Mendes storefront:

- Tux-USA formalwear: tuxedo jackets, tuxedo pants, gowns, dresses, wedding
  dresses, pocket squares.
- Stlouisbeautyline health and beauty products: acne cream, antiseptic wash,
  collagen, supplements.
- GoddessYou products outside the home-goods lane: phone case, ice roller,
  facial tools, hydration bottle, journal, satin sleep set, tech pouch.

## Product Families To Source Next

1. Sculptural table lamps and candle warmer lamps
   - Materials: travertine, alabaster-look stone, ceramic, frosted glass,
     smoked glass, aged brass, or warm metal.
   - Search phrases: "sculptural table lamp", "travertine lamp", "alabaster
     table lamp", "ceramic candle warmer lamp".

2. Handmade-style ceramics
   - Products: vases, low bowls, catchall trays, planters, tabletop objects.
   - Materials: terracotta, stoneware, matte ceramic, reactive glaze.
   - Search phrases: "matte ceramic vase", "terracotta bowl", "stoneware
     catchall tray", "reactive glaze planter".

3. Washed linen and tactile textiles
   - Products: cushion covers, throws, pillowcases, table runners, simple
     curtains.
   - Materials: washed linen, cotton slub, boucle, woven cotton, wool blend.
   - Search phrases: "washed linen cushion cover", "linen throw blanket",
     "cotton slub pillow cover", "neutral table runner".

4. Storage and reset objects
   - Products: woven baskets, drawer organizers, lidded boxes, trays, laundry
     baskets.
   - Materials: rattan, bamboo, seagrass, oak, linen-covered storage.
   - Search phrases: "woven storage basket", "seagrass tray", "drawer
     organizer set", "linen storage box".

5. Soft room accents
   - Products: picture frames, carved mirrors, small wall hooks, incense
     holders, ceramic candle holders.
   - Materials: wood, ceramic, stone, brushed metal, smoked glass.
   - Search phrases: "carved wood mirror", "ceramic candle holder", "stone
     incense holder", "minimal picture frame".

6. Quiet kitchen and dining objects
   - Products: oil dispensers, storage jars, serving boards, linen napkins,
     small organizers.
   - Keep these restrained and material-led so the catalog does not drift into
     gadget dropshipping.
   - Search phrases: "glass oil dispenser", "ceramic storage jar", "wood
     serving board", "linen napkins".

## Acceptance Criteria

- Product looks at home in a calm, neutral interior.
- No loud logos, novelty prints, medical claims, or unrelated personal-care use.
- Product photography is clean, high-resolution, and shows the item in a room.
- Supplier can provide reliable tracked shipping to target markets.
- Landed cost leaves enough margin after ads, payment fees, returns, and
  promotions.
- Variants are simple: color, size, scent, or material. Avoid complex sizing.
- Product description can be written around texture, ritual, material, and use.
