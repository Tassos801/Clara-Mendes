# Clara Mendes CJ Product Shortlist

Date: 2026-04-26

Working position: quiet, curated home goods for mindful rooms. The storefront currently leans into lighting, textiles, ceramics, storage, and accents, so this shortlist favors tactile, low-noise products over novelty items.

Assumption: initial market is US-facing. Final landed cost, shipping time, and availability need to be checked inside the connected CJ account because public CJ pages often hide exact delivery quotes until sign-in.

## Selection Rules

- Best first: textiles, trays, baskets, and simple decor with low compliance risk.
- Sample first: ceramics, heavier baskets, or products with inconsistent supplier photos.
- Hold for later: wired lighting, oversized furniture, seasonal novelty, and anything with weak brand fit.

## Hold For Later

- Wired wall lamps and bedside lamps: many CJ pages list 220V or electronics/battery attributes. For US-facing sales, launch these only after confirming plug type, certifications, safety labeling, and return risk.
- Furniture and large outdoor items: too bulky for the first Clara Mendes edit, with higher damage and return exposure.
- Novelty decor, acrylic wall art, animal-themed decor, and seasonal items: lower fit with the restrained Clara Mendes identity.

## CJ Checks Before Import

- Set ship-to country and confirm delivery estimate, shipping cost, and available warehouse.
- Check inventory and processing time for the exact variant, not only the parent product.
- Download images and remove supplier badges, watermarks, overlaid text, and poor lifestyle shots.
- Rename products into Clara Mendes language before publishing.
- For ceramics, ask CJ support about packaging and damage dispute evidence requirements.
- For textiles, order one sample and check hand feel, odor, stitching, color accuracy, and wash behavior.
